Sissel (シセル Sissel) is the protagonist of the game. He wakes up to see his corpse, and a woman being held at gunpoint. A ghost named Ray, who takes on the form of a desk lamp, guides him to save the woman, Lynne. He soon realises that he has lost his memory and sets out to find who he is and why he was killed. His powers include manipulating objects, travelling through telephone lines and replaying the last four minutes before death.
Name meaning: "Shiseru" is a Japanese word, meaning "dead"


In actuality, Sissel is a cat who belonged to the man he identified as himself, and his only friend for ten years. He was in the bag that Yomiel was carrying that night and was accidentally shot while Yomiel was manipulating Lynne. Though he later erases Yomiel's death, his past self ends up getting killed by the Temsik fragment and he gets to keep his ghost tricks as well as his body, which is frozen forever as a kitten.


Lynne (リンネ Lynne) is a rookie detective working alone on a case tonight. After being targeted by hitmen and saved by Sissel, she teams up with him so they can help each other with their individual tasks. She lives in an apartment with a little girl named Kamila and a dog named Missile.
Name meaning: "Rinne" is the Japanese word for Samsara, the cycle of death and rebirth.

The case she is working on involves a detective imprisoned for murder who she believes he is innocent. Ten years ago, she was taken hostage and was saved by Detective Jowd, whom she has looked up to ever since. Lynne is suspected of Sissel's murder and is even shown shooting him on a tape, but it turns out Yomiel was manipulating Lynne into shooting his "shell".


Ray (クネリ Kuneri) appears before Sissel as a desk lamp and teaches him about his ghost tricks. When Sissel realises he's lost his memory, Ray tells him to help out Lynne as she is the key to his memories. He also informs Sissel that he will disappear by daybreak.
Name meaning: "Kuneru" is a Japanese word meaning to wriggle/flex/bend.


In actuality, Ray is an older Missile from a timeline in which Lynne and Kamila died. When that timeline's Sissel refused to help him, being only interested in recovering his lost memory, Missile chased after Yomiel. Yomiel had been there in the apartment when Missile and Kamila were killed, inadvertently giving Missile ghost tricks. Missile ended up in the submarine's control room with Yomiel's corpse and went back ten years to the time of Yomiel's death. There, he waited until that fateful night came round so that he could coerce Sissel into helping him.


Kamila (カノン Kanon) is a young girl who lives with Lynne and Missile. She ends up in danger when a hitman comes to the apartment, but Sissel manages to save her. She then leaves to go and deliver an important music box to Lynne.
Name meaning: "Kannon" is the Japanese word for Guanyin, the bodhisattva associated with compassion. It is also a pun on "cannon", which fits with Missile.

Kamila is really the daughter of Detective Jowd. Five years ago, she was at home alone and built a birthday surprise for her mother. Unfortunately, Yomiel manipulated it into killing her mother and her father confessed to the crime to protect Kamila. When taking the music box to Lynne, she ends up getting kidnapped by the organization, because she was mistaken for the daughter of the justice minister.


Missile (ミサイル Missile) is Lynne and Kamila's pet Pomeranian, who Sissel has to save after he gets shot by a hitman. He is intensely loyal to his mistresses and swears to protect them. After bidding farewell to Sissel, he decides to make his own path.
Name meaning: Missile is named for the creator Shu Takumi's Pomeranian, also named Missile.

Missile manages to escape the apartment and catches up with Kamila in the park. When running after her, he is hit by a moped and falls into a crater where he dies again. This time though, he receives ghost tricks and uses them to save Kamila when she is crushed by Mino. Missile goes on to aid Sissel in averting other deaths. His powers include swapping objects and replaying the four minutes before a death.


Inspector Cabanela (カバネラ警部 Inspector Cabanela) is the head of the Special Investigation Unit. He is particularly fond of Lynne. It is rumoured that he only cares about his spotless record.
Name Meaning: His name is derived from "shikabane", the Japanese word for corpse.

Inspector Cabanela used to be friends with Detective Jowd until he was arrested for murder. After Jowd was arrested, Cabanela focused only on reaching the top of the ladder. He apprehends Jowd during his prison escape, making it appear he cares more about his spotless record than his old friend. It turns out that the reason he became head of the Special Investigation Unit was to oversee and control all aspects of the manipulator case in an effort to save Jowd, whom he truly believed to be innocent. He prevented his escape so that he wouldn't commit a crime. Ten years ago, Cabanela was involved in the incident when he was interrogating Yomiel and pushed him too far. He left his gun in the room by mistake, giving Yomiel the means to escape.


Jowd (ジョード Jowd) is one of the prisoners at the Special Prison. He was sent to jail for murdering his wife in front of a family member.
Name Meaning: "Joudo" is the Buddhist term for "pure land".

Jowd was the nation's top detective before turning himself in. After his daughter, Kamila's birthday surprise went horribly wrong and killed her mother, he confessed to murder for her sake. He initially desires execution, but changes his mind after learning about the manipulator and his daughter's kidnapping, leading to him pursuing the one truly responsible for his wife's death. Ten years ago, Jowd drove Yomiel to take a hostage and even decided to shoot him, something he has felt remorse over ever since.


The junkyard superintendent, nicknamed "Pigeon Man".

Formerly a coroner working for the police. He tried to perform the autopsy on Yomiel's corpse and failed. After the corpse vanished, he started investigating and found the link to the Temsik meteorite, which he studied ever since. Cabanela came to his office one day and the two teamed up to discover the secrets of the meteorite, the source of the manipulator's powers, and the truth behind what happened on the day Jowd's wife died.


The justice minister (オッチンドル Otchindoru) is a deeply troubled man who wants his wife and daughter to come back home.
Name Meaning: "Otchindoru" comes from the Japanese phrase "Osshindoru" meaning "Oh! He's dead".

Signed the order for Jowd's execution despite opposing the death penalty. It turned out Yomiel had manipulated him. The organization even kidnaps his daughter to ensure it isn't reversed. His wife moved out after learning the truth. After discovering that his daughter is in fact safe and receiving the important evidence of Jowd's innocence, he calls off the execution order.


Emma (エンマ Emma) is the wife of the Justice Minister, who moved out with her daughter and refuses to answer her husband's calls. She works frantically on her novel through the night, trying to complete it before the deadline. Barking dogs are a great nuisance to her.
Name Meaning: "Enma" is the Buddhist term for Yama, the lord of death.

She actually moved out because her husband wouldn't tell everyone the truth about why he signed the execution order and then withdraw it.


Amelie (エイミン Eimin) is the daughter of Emma and the Justice Minister. She is sick with a fever and unable to go out to her lesson tonight.
Name Meaning: "Eimin" means "eternal sleep" in Japanese.

Amelie was actually supposed to be kidnapped by the organization, but because of her fever, she evades capture.

The Manipulator
This mysterious person is involved in many strange things happening in this town tonight.


Yomiel (ヨミエル Yomiel) is the true owner of the body Sissel mistakes as his. His ghost powers include manipulating objects and living things/corpses, and travelling through phone lines.
Name Meaning: Yomiel comes from the Japanese word "yomigaeru", meaning to revive.

Ten years ago, Yomiel was a top systems programmer. He was invited onto a project involving national secrets and ended up being suspected of being a spy. Cabanela interrogated him harshly, causing him to lose hope then accidentally left his gun, giving Yomiel the means to escape. Jowd chased him down, driving him to take Lynne hostage and end up being struck down by a fragment of a falling meteorite. Yomiel received ghost tricks and his body became a shell trapped between life and death.

After dying, Yomiel lived in the body of a stray kitten before recovering his memories. He retrieved his body and hurried to see his fiancee, only to find she had ended her life to join him. Yomiel named the kitten Sissel after her. A lonely existence followed, driving Yomiel to kill Jowd's wife and make Jowd feel the same pain. Years later, Yomiel sought out revenge against those he blamed for his death, with the assistance of an organization. He made a deal with them, the source of his powers and his co-operation in erasing those who knew of Temsik in exchange for a new life.

The organization ended up betraying Yomiel and stealing the Temsik fragment. He abandons his plans for revenge and travels into the past with Sissel, Missile and Jowd. Together, they create a new ten years where he lives.
