Commander Sith (シス司令 Commander Sith) is responsible for ordering the hit on Lynne. He seems to work for an organization.
Name Meaning: "Sith" comes from the Japanese word "shisu", meaning to die.
Sith struck a deal with Yomiel, promising a new life in exchange for the fragment. He was actually plotting to betray Yomiel and steal the fragment all along. Sith destroys the submarine, leaving Yomiel stranded in the sea along with everyone else, even going so far as to separate him from his body.


Commander Sith's adjutant who performs various tasks for him by banging on a control panel.

He is left in the command room with Jowd when it is ejected from the submarine. When Jowd points out he is trapped too, he reveals he is a remote controlled robot, before gunning Jowd down.


Nearsighted Jeego (ド近眼のジーゴ Nearsighted Jeego) is the first hitman who comes to kill Lynne.
Name Meaning: "jigoku" is Japanese for "hell".

He is killed when Sissel drops a wrecking ball on him and it rolls away never to be seen again.


"One Step Ahead" Tengo (先回りのテンゴ One Step Ahead Tengo) is the second hitman assigned to kill Lynne. He comes into her apartment, shoots Missile and ties Kamila up.
Name Meaning: "tengoku" is Japanese for "heaven".

When he learns Lynne is still at the junkyard, he heads there to kill her. Sissel disposes of him by dropping a heavy crate on his head.


Beauty (ビューティー Beauty) is one half of a suspicious couple dining at the Chicken Kitchen.

She is actually working for the organization and is involved in the kidnapping of the justice minister's daughter. It seems that she can sense the powers of the dead, though this is never elaborated on.


Dandy (ダンディー Dandy) is the other half of the suspicious couple.

He is working with Beauty on the kidnapping. Dandy runs over Missile in the park and carries out the kidnapping of Kamila.


The medical examiner who comes to look over Sissel's body.

He is a fake medical examiner who is actually supposed to take Yomiel's "shell" away and meet up with him later to return it but Cabanela figures him out and bribes him to smuggle the body to the superintendent's office instead.


A minor crew hand working on the submarine.


The police chief.


Detective Rindge (リンジュ刑事 Detective Rindge) is performing a stakeout in the park on Cabanela's orders.
Name Meaning: "rinju" is a Japanese word meaning deathbed.

He is actually staking out "Point X" (The Chicken Kitchen), waiting for the manipulator to show up. When Lynne shows up instead, he races there and ends up in a fatal accident which Sissel is able to avert. Spends the rest of the night looking out for Kamila at Lynne's request.


Memry (メメリ Memry) is a waitress who works at The Chicken Kitchen.
Name Meaning: Her name comes from the Latin phrase "memento mori", meaning "remember you will die".

In actuality, she is an undercover detective. She causes a major catastrophe by planting a bug on the chicken she brings to the suspicious couple. According to Rindge, she is known as the "Lynne" of the Special Investigation Unit.


Detective McCaw (マッコー刑事 Detective McCaw) is one of the detectives who attend the crime scene.
Name Meaning: His name comes from "makkou", the Japanese word for incense powder.


The other detective who attends the crime scene.
