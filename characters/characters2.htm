Commander Sith (シス司令 Commander Sith) is responsible for ordered the hit on Lynne. He seems to work for an organization.
Name Meaning: "Sith" comes from the Japanese word "shisu", meaning to die.
He has a deal going on with Yomiel, promising him a new life in exchange for the Temsik fragment. However, it turns out he was actually planning to betray Yomiel and steal the Temsik fragment all along. He does so, and even destroys his own submarine, leaving Yomiel stranded along with Sissel, Missile, Lynne and Kamila. He also has the control room ejected from the submarine with Detective Jowd and Yomiel's body in it, hoping to prevent any small possibility from happening.


Commander Sith's servant who performs various tasks for him by banging on a control panel.

Sith's servant He is left in the command room when it is ejected and it turns out that he is in fact a remote controlled robot.


Nearsigted Jeego (ド近眼のジーゴ Nearsighted Jeego) is the first hitman who comes to kill Lynne.
Name Meaning: "jigoku" is Japanese for "hell".

He is killed when Sissel drops a wrecking ball on him and it rolls away never to be seen again.


"One Step Ahead" Tengo (先回りのテンゴ One Step Ahead Tengo) is the second hitman assigned to kill Lynne. He comes into her apartment, shoots Missile dead and ties Kamila up.
Name Meaning: "tengoku" is Japanese for "heaven".

When he learns Lynne is still at the junkyard, he heads there to kill her. Sissel disposes of him by dropping a heavy crate on his head.


Beauty (ビューティー Beauty) is one half of a suspicious couple dining at the Chicken Kitchen.

She is actually working for the organization and is involved in the kidnapping of the justice minister's daughter. It seems that she can sense the powers of the dead, though this is never elaborated on.


Dandy (ダンディー Dandy) is the other half of the suspicious couple.

He is working with Beauty on the kidnapping. Dandy runs over Missile in the park, leading to his getting ghost tricks, and carries out the kidnapping of Kamila.


The medical examiner who comes to look over Sissel's body.

He is a fake medical examiner who is actually supposed to take Yomiel's "shell" away and meet up with him later to return it but Cabanela figures him out and bribes him to smuggle the body to the superintendent's office instead.

Detective Rindge (リンジュ刑事 Detective Rindge) is performing a stakeout in the park on Cabanela's orders.
Name Meaning: "rinju" is a Japanese word meaning deathbed.

He is actually staking out "Point X" (The Chicken Kitchen) for the manipulator who is due to show up. He has a fatal accident when driving over there while listening to a bug, but Sissel averts his fate. He spends the rest of the night looking out for Kamila at Lynne's request.


Memry (メメリ Memry) is a waitress who works at The Chicken Kitchen.
Name Meaning: Her name comes from the Latin phrase "memento mori", meaning "remember you will die".

In actuality, she is an undercover detective. She bugs the chicken she's going to deliver to the blue couple which ends up causing a major catastrophe. According to Rindge, she is known as the "Lynne" of the Special Investigation Unit.


Detective McCaw (マッコー刑事 Detective McCaw) is one of the detectives who attend the crime scene.
Name Meaning: His name comes from "makko", the Japanese word for incense powder.
