Hello, and welcome to Temsik Park, the first dedicated fansite created for the fantastic Nintendo DS/iOS game, Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective!

This site aims to be a top resource for fans of the game. You can find all sorts of Ghost Trick related media here, and it serves as an useful source of information about the game as well.

If you have stumbled upon this site by accident or are just looking to find something out about the game in the event that you're curious and have yet to play it, then tread carefully. I try to be careful about spoilers but you may end up accidentally spoiling yourself - and Ghost Trick is something you do not want to spoil. If you haven't played it yet, then you are seriously missing out.

Ghost Trick is a wonderful game from the creator of Ace Attorney with a vivid cast of characters and masterful storytelling as well as a great soundtrack. I would recommend it to anybody. Then you can come back and take a look around the site.

Have fun looking around and enjoy your stay!
