About the site

Ghost Trick has a rather small fandom and while you can find some places here and there to discuss it or show off your creativity or whatnot, there actually wasn't a dedicated fansite for it yet. I felt it needed a site that could accumulate information and media relating to it and put it all together that could serve as a valuable resource and so I took on the duty of making a Ghost Trick site. Originally, I registered the domain "ghosttrick.net" and just called the site "Ghost Trick", but then I realised it was a bit lazy and unoriginal. I wanted it to have a proper name, so I decided to call it "Temsik Park", after a very important location in the game, and registered a new domain for the site. So now, here we are. The first site was launched in late July 2011 and then the second site came in August 2011. Yes, I did change my mind about it very quickly, didn't I? After a few years of absence from the fandom, the site was reuploaded to Github.

About the webmistress

I'm Caz, a girl who likes to game, mostly JRPGs and visual novel style games though I do partake in the odd walking simulator or adventure game too. As a fan of the Ace Attorney games, it only seemed natural that I would be into another game from Shu Takumi himself. I ended up loving the game enough to go and make an entire site for it. I've written a ton of fanfiction for it too, so if you're into reading fanfiction, you can check it out on Archive of our Own, under the penname kyarorain.
