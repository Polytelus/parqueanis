So far, there have been a total of two artbooks released for Ghost Trick.





The first one is the Ghost Trick Official Complete Guide. It contains walkthroughs for the game, character models and portraits, relationship charts and other miscellaneous things. It can be purchased physically or digitally. This download is the digital version, with all pages.

The second one is the Capcom Special Selection Ghost Trick artbook. Features portraits and character models, including some not found in the Official Complete Guide, and some other artwork. This was released with a mini soundtrack CD and a post card. It was limited edition, so much harder to find than the Official Complete Guide unfortunately.
