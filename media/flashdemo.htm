The Ghost Trick flash demo. If you cannot see anything, your browser may not have Flash enabled.

As of 2021, Adobe has tragically killed off Flash. This page will remain up for those who are lucky enough to have browser workarounds.

In the meantime, there are some ways to still view Flash files. One method you can try is downloading Flash Player Projector. It is a standalone program and does not require installation. The SWF file can be downloaded here.
